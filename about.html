Movies are a form of visual storytelling , and they are categorized into various types or genres based on narrative elements, themes and emotional effects. The main types of movies include:

Action movies

Focuses on exciting sequences like chases and combat(e.g., Die Hard, Commando).

Comedy movies

Intended to make the audience laugh(e.g.,Some Like It Hot, 3Idiots).

Romance movies

Focuseson romantic relationships and emotions(e.g.,Caseabalanca).

scientific movies

Centers on futuristic technology and speculative science(e.g., Star War).

This classification helps audience know what kind of story experience to expect and aids filmmakers in shaping their narrative and style.
